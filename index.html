About

I am a member of Microsystems Design Lab (MDL) at Penn State, where I work with my advisor Dr. Mahmut T. Kandemir.

My research interests include: computer architecture and operating systems. More specifically, I am interested in researching the memory-subsystem (including LLCs, DRAM [stacked and 2D]) optimizations through (1) hardware-only, (2) software-only and (3) hardware-software co-design optimizations for large and many-core systems. I have research experience working on CPU micro-architecture and parallel-programming as well. Please refer to my papers/resume for more information on the relevant experience.

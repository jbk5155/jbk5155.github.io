About Me

I am a researcher at AMD, working on optimizing CPU, heterogeneous CPU/GPU systems. My research interests include: computer architecture and operating systems. More specifically, I am interested in optimizing the cache/memory subsystems through (1) hardware-only, (2) software(OS/hypervisor)-only and (3) hardware-software co-design approaches. Apart from memory systems, in the past I have also worked on the CPU micro-architecture. Before joining AMD, I got my PhD from Penn State (in 2017) where I was working with Dr. Mahmut T. Kandemir.

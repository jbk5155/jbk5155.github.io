About

I am was a member of Microsystems Design Lab (MDL) at Penn State, where I was working with Dr. Mahmut T. Kandemir.

My research interests include: computer architecture and operating systems. More specifically, I am interested in researching the memory-subsystem (including LLCs, DRAM [stacked and 2D]) optimizations in large and many-core systems through (1) hardware-only, (2) software(OS/hypervisor)-only and (3) hardware-software co-design approaches. I have research experience working on CPU micro-architecture and parallel-programming as well. Please refer to my papers for more information on the relevant experience.

Updated resume(including papers/patents under submission/preparation) will be provided upon request.
